ROADMAP

BCA

BCA is a bachelor's degree in computer applications, emphasizing software development and computer science topics like programming, databases, and web development. It prepares students for careers in the IT industry by combining theory and practical skills.

BBA

BBA is a bachelor's degree in business administration, offering a broad understanding of business principles like finance, marketing, and management. It prepares students for entry-level business roles or further studies in specialized areas of business.

B.COM

BCom stands for Bachelor of Commerce. It's an undergraduate degree program that focuses on commerce, accounting, finance, economics, and business studies. BCom provides students with a comprehensive understanding of various aspects of business and commerce,

LLB

LLB is a bachelor's degree in law, focusing on legal principles and systems like constitutional law, criminal law, and contracts. It equips students with critical thinking and analytical skills for careers in law or related fields.

ENGINEERING

Engineering involves applying scientific and technical knowledge to design, develop, and maintain structures, machines, systems, and materials. It spans various disciplines and industries, requiring problem-solving skills and innovation to address real-world challenges.

MEDICINE

Medical education trains individuals to become healthcare professionals, like doctors. It involves academic study, clinical training, and specialization, preparing them to diagnose, treat, and prevent illnesses and injuries.
